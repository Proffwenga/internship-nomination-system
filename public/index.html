HOW IT WORKS

Get your nominations in, in six steps

1

Register your institution

Create a placement-officer account using your institution email address.

2

Verify your email

Open the verification link we send you, then log in with your new password.

3

Review the cohort details

Check the Background section for eligibility rules, key dates and the nomination limit.

4

Add your institution details

Enter your Dean or HOD's name, job title, phone and email, then complete the declarations.

5

Nominate your students

Add up to 10 students one by one, or upload the Excel template. At least 4 must be vernacular media placements.

6

Submit before the deadline

Confirm your declarations and submit by 25 July 2026. Shortlisted candidates are contacted for interviews on 07 August 2026.
